Refund Policy

At AutoParts Direct, we are committed to ensuring customer satisfaction by providing high-quality products and reliable service. We understand that sometimes returns and refunds are necessary, and we aim to make the process as simple and transparent as possible.

If you are not completely satisfied with your purchase, you may request a refund within 30 days of the purchase date. To qualify for a refund, the item must be in its original condition, unused, and returned in the original packaging. Please include a copy of your receipt or proof of purchase to facilitate the return process.

Refunds will be processed once the returned item has been received and inspected. If the return meets our requirements, a refund will be issued to your original payment method within 5-10 business days. Please note that shipping fees are non-refundable, and customers are responsible for return shipping costs unless the return is due to a product defect or an error on our part.

Certain items may not be eligible for refunds, including clearance items, special-order products, and electronic components that have been opened or installed. If you receive a defective or incorrect item, please contact our support team within 7 days of receiving your order to arrange for a replacement or refund.
